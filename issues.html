Issues through time

Introduction to the Issues

The Issues represent historical-cultural ages, allowing to get in touch with the evolution of poetry through time. Three issues are proposed at the moment, Ancient Era, XIXth century and XXIst century (nowadays), but more are coming soon! For each cultural era, you will find one poetess, with three of her poems.
